Introduction

My name is Jacob Faile. I am an Atlanta, GA based research engineer at Georgia Tech Research Institute (GTRI) in the Electro-Optical Systems Laboratory (EOSL). I have a Master of Science in Robotics Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology and a Bachelor of Science in Mechatronics Engineering from Kennesaw State University. My interests include activities like musical theater, table top games, hiking, and kayaking. Though these activities will always bring a lot of joy to me, engineering is certainly the passion I want to devote my career to.

Biography and Objectives

Biography

Jacob Faile was born in Kentucky in 1998. He spent most of his childhood on the east coast of the USA moving around Kentucky, Indiana, and Georgia. His family eventually settled in Rome, Georgia and Jacob completed his high school education dual enrolling as a home schools student and a college student at Georgia Northwestern Technical College. After graduation, he enrolled in the pre-engineering pathway at Georgia Highlands College to complete all his core engineering core classes. During this time, Jacob Faile worked at the Chick-Fil-A Dwarf House in Rome, GA as a server. He also worked as a runner for a law firm called Brinson Askew Berry in Rome, GA to help pay for his classes. During this time Jacob spent a lot of his free time performing on stage and working backstage at Rome Little Theatre.

After completing his core classes at Georgia Highlands, Jacob transferred to the Southern Poly Tech campus of Kennesaw State University to begin his specialization in Mechatronics Engineering. Jacob started working at the Chick-Fil-A Dwarf House in Woodstock, GA to assist in paying his way through college. He also took on a Teaching Assistant roles at Kennesaw State University teaching classes and labs like Intermediate Programming for Mechatronics, Mechatronics Engineering Fundamentals, and Advanced Controls. His work at Chick-Fil-A and his work as a TA gave him scholarships that allowed him to continue working his education at KSU.

Jacob graduated Summa Cum Laude from KSU with a 4.0 GPA and quickly entered the work force. He took a job at Georgia Tech Research Institute in the Electro-Optical Systems Lab as an algorithm and analysis researcher working on both classified and unclassified DoD, DoW projects and private sponsored projects. He focused heavily on new research in robotics, algorithms, machine learning, AI, and electro-optical and LiDAR systems.

During this time, Jacob started his Masters Degree in Robotics at Georgia Tech. He also began performing again at various theatre companies in the North Atlanta area. Most recently he performed in roles such as the Beast in Beauty and Beast and Davey in Newsies.

During his Masters Degree, Jacob begun working with Dr. Fan Zhang in the Intelligence for Advanced Nuclear lab at Georgia Tech. He advanced their Robotics and AI research by assisting with a nuclear flow loop test bed and developing a new Robotic Sensor Apparatus for autonomous plant monitoring and alerting. This RSA system ended up being his Masters Capstone Project. Jacob then graduated from Georgia Tech in December of 2025 with a 3.9 GPA.

Jacob currently lives in the North Atlanta area and spends his free time playing Dungeons and Dragons with friends and coworkers, camping and hiking with his wife and son, and working on side projects in the robotics space.

Objectives

I am a researcher and engineer open to new opportunities utilizing my skills in Robotics and Computer Science. I have over 8 years of technical experience in engineering topics such as robotics, machine learning, computer vision, automation, control systems, embedded systems, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and computer science. My skillset centers around the integration of these various engineering disciplines, the quick learning and implementation of new concepts (software, instrumentation, and theory), and a focus on designing various mechanical systems, electrical systems, and computer systems. I am continually developing these passions and delving into my field with an eagerness to learn as much as I can.
